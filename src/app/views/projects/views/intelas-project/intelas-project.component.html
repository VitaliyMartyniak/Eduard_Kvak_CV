Scope

The primary objective was to create an MVP solution for managers to collect, standardize, and manage real estate data. This solution aimed to demonstrate capabilities and attract investment for further product development. My responsibilities::

Conducting user research to gain insights into user needs, behaviors, and preferences.
Performing competitor analysis to identify industry standards and opportunities.
Developing product architecture to ensure scalability and efficiency.
Creating detailed designs and prototypes.
Challenges
Developing intuitive table and report builders.
Designing tables with complex nesting levels.
Implementing advanced filtering and sorting mechanisms.
Providing comprehensive descriptions for data calculations.
Working process

Explore the various stages below to gain insight into our approach and methodologies.

We started by conducting in-depth user interviews and surveys to uncover critical insights into user needs, behaviors, and pain points. We performed a thorough competitor analysis to spot market trends and gaps. In the ideation phase, creativity and collaboration took center stage. We hosted dynamic brainstorming sessions and workshops with key stakeholders to generate a broad spectrum of ideas and solutions. We sketched and created high-fidelity wireframes to explore various design decisions. We then prioritized ideas based on their feasibility, potential impact, and alignment with the project's objectives. In the design phase, we turned our top ideas into detailed and polished designs. We created a UI Kit to ensure the design was consistent and efficient across the project. During the prototyping phase, we brought our designs to life. We developed an interactive, functional prototype that not only simulated the user experience but also highlighted key functionalities. This allowed stakeholders to engage with a tangible representation of the final product, providing invaluable feedback for further refinement.
Result

Streamlined data management

The platform successfully standardized and centralized real estate data, making it easier for managers to collect and work with the information.

Advanced features implementation

Clear and easy to read table, ability so swap columns with rows, add/remove/pin table columns, report builders, advanced filtering and sorting capabilities, and more.

Effective prototyping

The functional prototype effectively demonstrated the product's capabilities, garnering positive feedback from investors. That helped stakeholders secure additional funding for further development and scaling of the product.
