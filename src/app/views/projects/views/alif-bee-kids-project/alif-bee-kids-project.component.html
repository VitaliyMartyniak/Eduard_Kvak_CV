Scope

The main goal was to redesign the registration flow, addressing the high dropout rate among users. My responsibilities included:

Conducting user research to identify pain points and user needs.
Performing a thorough competitor analysis.
Designing a new registration flow aligned with the company’s branding and style guidelines.
Challenges
Multiple language support.
Reducing the number of steps required for registration.
Working process

Explore the various stages below to gain insight into our approach and methodologies.

Given the tight schedule, we focused our research on quickly gathering critical insights. We utilized analytics to pinpoint user pain points and identify areas where users were dropping off during the registration process. This data-driven approach allowed us to understand user behavior and preferences efficiently. Additionally, we conducted a thorough competitor analysis to understand industry standards and identify opportunities for improvement. These insights informed our design strategy and guided the development of a more user-friendly registration flow. During ideation, we held intensive brainstorming sessions to generate a variety of ideas and potential solutions. We collaborated closely with stakeholders to create and refine wireframes, exploring different design directions. We prioritized ideas based on feasibility, user impact, and alignment with project goals. In the design phase, we translated our top ideas into high-fidelity designs. We focused on creating a seamless and user-friendly registration flow, ensuring all elements were consistent with the company’s visual identity. Upon completion, we prepared comprehensive design documentation and resources for the development team, ensuring a smooth handover and facilitating efficient implementation of the new registration flow.
Result

Enhanced registration flow

The new, streamlined registration process addressed the identified pain points, making it more intuitive and user-friendly.

Social network integration

We successfully added the ability for users to sign in via social networks, offering greater convenience and flexibility.

Reduced drop-off rate

As a result of these improvements, the user drop-off rate during registration was reduced to less than 10%.
