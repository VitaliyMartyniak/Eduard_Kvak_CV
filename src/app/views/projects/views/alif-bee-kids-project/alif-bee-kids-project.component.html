AlifBee Kids

AlifBee Kids is an engaging mobile platform that teaches children the Arabic language through interactive games. The platform is designed to make language learning fun and effective, catering specifically to young learners. By combining educational content with playful elements, AlifBee Kids aims to enhance language acquisition and retention, providing a valuable resource for parents and educators.

Overview

The main objective was to redesign the registration flow to reduce the high dropout rate among users and improve the overall user experience.

Responsibilities

Conducting user research to identify pain points and user needs.
Performing a thorough competitor analysis.
Designing a new registration flow aligned with company branding and style guidelines.

Challenges

Multiple language support.
Reducing the number of registration steps.
Reduce the high dropout rate among users.

These insights informed our design strategy and guided the development of a more user-friendly registration flow.
