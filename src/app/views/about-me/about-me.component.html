About me

I've been immersed in the dynamic intersection of product design and technology for over 6 years. My journey began during my studies at Lviv Polytechnic University, where I delved into the realms of tech and design within the Computer Engineering faculty.

Driven by a combination of research insights and quantitative data, my approach to design is rooted in empathy for users while strategically aligning with business objectives.

To continuously evolve my expertise in business and customer experience design, I actively pursue further education through various courses, lectures, and extensive reading across diverse design disciplines, applying these insights directly to my projects.

Beyond my individual pursuits, I'm deeply committed to fostering growth within the design community. As a mentor, I guide aspiring designers, sharing knowledge and insights to empower them in their journey. Additionally, I create content and deliver lectures, contributing to our team's collective knowledge base and fostering a culture of collaboration and innovation.
